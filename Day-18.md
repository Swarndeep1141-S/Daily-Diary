### Summary

Today, I learned about **Hyperparameter Tuning**, a technique used to find the best combination of model settings before training a machine learning model. The trainer explained the difference between **parameters** and **hyperparameters** and discussed important hyperparameters for **Decision Tree, Random Forest, KNN, and SVM** models.

During the practical session, we split the dataset into training and testing sets and trained a **Decision Tree Classifier**. We then applied **GridSearchCV** and **RandomizedSearchCV** to identify the best hyperparameters for the Decision Tree model. Finally, we used **GridSearchCV** to optimize a **Random Forest Classifier** and compared the performance of different parameter combinations to improve model accuracy.

### Key Learning
